Welcome to the Automation Projects

This is a project to automate the process of creating contracts and invoices.
The project is divided into four modules, each with its own page.
Click on the cards below to access the respective pages.

PDF TOOLS

This PDF Reader enables specific or full-page reading and lets you remove excess pages for streamlined document review.

Quotation Generator

A Quotation Generator tool that quickly creates customized quotes for products or services, enabling users to input details, adjust pricing, and generate professional, ready-to-share documents in minutes.

Paginator and Initializer

A Paginator and Initializer tool for numbering and signing pages, providing an efficient way to format documents with consistent page numbers and signatures.

Excel to PDF

An Excel to PDF converter tool that quickly transforms spreadsheets into professional PDF documents, preserving formatting and ensuring easy sharing and printing.
